What is Hustler's University ?

Hustler's University 3.0 is a worldwide community of over 110,000+ active members learning from a select network of experts in freelancing, crypto, investing, and business. Founded in 2021 by Andrew 'Cobra' Tate, we grow stronger every day while traditional education becomes less and less relevant

Who is Andrew Tate ?

Andrew Tate is the founder of Hustlers University and a four-time world champion in kickboxing. He has gained notoriety recently on social media. Andrew Tate has a passion for teaching and started Hustlers University because he saw the need to provide accessible education on modern wealth creation techniques.
